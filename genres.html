In the world of cinema there are several genres, which are classified every day, it is more easier if we search on the Internet for a page of films.

  1. Drama

    This genre is based on telling stories, often based on real events in which a person or a group of them have to go through various circumstances.

  2. Comedy

    These films are designed to make the audience laugh through amusement and most often work by exaggerating characteristics for humorous effect. Films in this style traditionally have a happy ending...

  3. Terror

    Horror Films are unsettling films designed to frighten and panic, cause dread and alarm, and to invoke our hidden worst fears, often in a terrifying, shocking finale, while captivating and entertaining us at the same time in a cathartic experience.

  4. Science/Fiction

    Science fiction film (or sci-fi film) is a genre that uses speculative, fictional science-based depictions of phenomena that are not fully accepted by mainstream science, such as extraterrestrial lifeforms, alien worlds, extrasensory perception and time travel, along with futuristic elements such as spacecraft, robots
